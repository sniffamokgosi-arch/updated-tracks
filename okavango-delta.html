Okavango Delta Explorer

Journey through the world's largest inland delta, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, home to incredible biodiversity and stunning landscapes

5 Days / 4 Nights
Small Groups (Max 8)
Water-Based Safari

Discover the Jewel of the Kalahari

The Okavango Delta is one of Africa's most spectacular wildlife destinations. This unique ecosystem transforms the Kalahari Desert into a lush, water-filled paradise teeming with life. Our Okavango Delta Explorer safari takes you deep into this UNESCO World Heritage Site for an unforgettable water-based adventure.

Experience the Delta from traditional mokoros (dugout canoes), walk with expert guides through pristine wilderness, and witness incredible wildlife including elephants, hippos, crocodiles, and over 400 bird species. Our carefully crafted itinerary combines adventure with comfort, staying in intimate tented camps that bring you close to nature.

Mokoro Excursions

Glide silently through crystal-clear channels in traditional dugout canoes, getting up close to wildlife and aquatic birds.

Walking Safaris

Explore the Delta on foot with experienced armed guides, learning to track animals and identify flora and fauna.

Bird Watching Paradise

With over 400 species, the Delta is a birdwatcher's dream, including rare sightings like Pel's Fishing Owl.

Photography Opportunities

Capture stunning landscapes and wildlife in perfect light conditions with guidance from our photography-aware guides.

Delta Wildlife

Encounter the incredible biodiversity of the Okavango Delta

Elephants

Large herds of African elephants roam the Delta, often seen crossing channels and feeding on aquatic plants.

Hippos

The Delta supports one of Africa's largest hippo populations, best viewed from mokoros during the day.

Bird Life

Over 400 species including fish eagles, kingfishers, herons, and the rare Pel's Fishing Owl.

Predators

Lions, leopards, wild dogs, and hyenas are regularly sighted, especially during dry season.
