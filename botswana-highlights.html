Botswana Highlights Combo

Experience the ultimate Botswana safari combining the Okavango Delta, Chobe National Park, and the Kalahari Desert in one unforgettable journey

10 Days / 9 Nights
Small Groups (Max 8)
3 Iconic Destinations

Ultimate Botswana Experience

This comprehensive safari package combines Botswana's three most spectacular destinations into one epic journey. Experience the world's largest inland delta, Africa's largest elephant population, and the vast Kalahari Desert with expert guides and premium accommodations.

From mokoro rides in the Okavango Delta to river cruises in Chobe and desert walks in the Kalahari, this safari offers unparalleled diversity. You'll witness Botswana's iconic wildlife, stunning landscapes, and authentic cultural experiences, all while traveling in small groups for personalized attention.

Okavango Delta

Mokoro rides, walking safaris, and incredible water-based wildlife viewing in the UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Chobe National Park

River cruises and game drives to witness Africa's largest concentration of elephants and diverse wildlife.

Kalahari Desert

Desert adapted wildlife, San cultural experiences, and breathtaking sunsets in the ancient Kalahari.

Premium Service

Expert local guides, luxury accommodations, and personalized attention throughout your journey.

Detailed Itinerary

Day-by-day breakdown of your Botswana Highlights adventure

1
Arrival in Maun & Okavango Delta Transfer
Maun → Okavango Delta

Arrive at Maun International Airport where you'll be greeted by your TRACKS guide. After a brief orientation, transfer to your first camp in the Okavango Delta. Settle in and enjoy your first African sunset with a welcome drink.

Airport Transfer
Camp Orientation

Okavango Delta Exploration
Okavango Delta

Two full days exploring the Okavango Delta by mokoro (traditional canoe) and on foot. Experience incredible water-based wildlife viewing, bird watching, and walking safaris with expert local guides. Evenings around the campfire under African skies.

Mokoro Excursions
Walking Safaris
Bird Watching

Chobe National Park Adventures
Chobe National Park

Transfer to Chobe National Park, home to Africa's largest elephant population. Enjoy morning and afternoon game drives, sunset river cruises on the Chobe River, and exceptional predator viewing opportunities including lion, leopard, and wild dog.

Game Drives
River Cruises

Kalahari Desert Experience
Central Kalahari

Journey into the vast Kalahari Desert. Experience the unique ecosystem and specially adapted wildlife. Visit a San Bushmen community to learn about their ancient culture and survival skills. Enjoy spectacular sunsets and stargazing in one of Africa's last true wilderness areas.
