The TRACKS Story

Tracks Adventure Safaris & Lodges is a 100% Botswana owned company incorporated under the companies act in Maun, situated in the North West District. We were formed to satisfy the needs of fellow countrymen and international travelers by providing exceptional facility management services and assisting the government in its mandate of public-private partnership.

Located in the new mall at plot 361 along the airport road opposite the museum, we are a business led by a dedicated, hardworking and passionate team fully owned by Ms. Onalenna Phillimon. Our mission is to bridge the gap in the mobile safari business by offering high quality, reasonably priced safari experiences entirely owned and operated by Batswana.

We believe in sustainable tourism that benefits local communities while providing unforgettable experiences for our guests. Our team consists of experienced guides who have grown up in these regions and possess deep knowledge of Botswana's ecosystems, wildlife, and cultures.

Our Purpose

Driven by passion, guided by principles

Our Mission

To provide authentic, sustainable safari experiences that showcase Botswana's natural wonders while supporting local communities and conservation efforts. We aim to bridge the gap in the mobile safari industry by offering high-quality, reasonably priced adventures owned and operated by Batswana, ensuring every guest experiences the true heart of Africa.

Our Vision

To be recognized as Botswana's premier locally-owned safari operator, known for exceptional service, authentic experiences, and meaningful contributions to wildlife conservation and community development. We envision a future where tourism benefits both visitors and host communities equally, preserving Africa's wilderness for generations to come.
